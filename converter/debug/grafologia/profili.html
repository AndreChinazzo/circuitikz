Be it a gift for someone close to you, or rather a means of finding out something about yourself, a personality portrait based on a graphological analysis is often an occasion for introspection and for getting to know someone better.

The graphologist strives to give people insights and knowledge, but with a narrative rather than a psychological language.

Each personality has a thousand facets, and the graphologist can not see and describe them all. The portrait, hence, focuses on the few essential aspects — those that shape all the rest.

Undestanding how these colorful threads combine to form the complicated tapestry that is everyone's personality is a useful tool to best descrive be overall picture.
